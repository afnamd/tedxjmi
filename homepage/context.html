Our idea of "Different Lenses"
When we're talking about different lenses, we mean different eyes, various individuals, and every individual is unique in their way, made uniquely, would see things differently and perceive it based on their ideology and thinking process, which may also include the experiences they've had in the past. Since no two occasions can be the same, no two views will ever match entirely. It is imperative to look beyond our perspective and understand that everyone is different (and hence has a distinctive perspective) because only then can a society live in peace and harmony. Understanding that we're all very different will also make the world a more empathetic and better place to live in, I firmly believe so. So herewith this theme, we will bring the seen and the unseen world together for everyone to see the horizon of perspectives.
The TEDx Conference
TEDxJMI brings together a collection of doers, thinkers, innovators, explorers, visionaries, teachers and learners We seek to illuminate, inspire, change perceptions, incite action and foster new connections. We are focused on proffering a TED stage to speakers of varied backgrounds and experiences for inspirational and intellectual acceleration. In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program for local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection.
